About LeafGuard

LeafGuard is an AI-powered tool that helps farmers detect rice leaf diseases with speed, accuracy, and ease. Our mission is to empower communities with accessible agricultural technology that promotes healthier crops and better yields.

🌱 Our Mission

To provide farmers with intelligent tools that simplify crop monitoring and disease management, improving food security worldwide.

🤖 AI-Powered

Using machine learning, LeafGuard identifies diseases from leaf images and provides instant, reliable results in multiple languages.

🌍 Community Focused

Designed with farmers in mind, LeafGuard is lightweight, multilingual, and accessible even in low-resource environments.
